About

Background

I'm from Belém, Pará — a curious kid who fell in love with data and never looked back. That curiosity took me from UNICAMP to a research lab in Belgium, through Nestlé's factory floors, a postdoc at USP on barley genetics, and eventually into data science leadership.

Today I coordinate a Data Science team at Spectral Solutions, where I build ML models that turn invisible signals into real decisions — and I'm still as excited about a good dataset as I was on day one.

My work sits at the intersection of applied ML, spectroscopy, and product development. I'm currently expanding into NLP, LLMs, and financial risk modeling — because the next interesting problem is always worth chasing.
